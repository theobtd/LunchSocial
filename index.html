Frequently Asked Questions

How does Apero.Social work?
When you sign up, you'll be paired with two other individuals at a designated location, date, and time. It's not an event; rather, we simply make a reservation for you. The two others are strangers to each other, creating a relaxed atmosphere for genuine interaction. Just show up, chat, and enjoy your hour together!
What is NOT Apero.Social?
Apero.Social is NOT a place for professional networking, finding a partner, or heavy drinking. Moreover, keep in mind that the point of Apero.Social is to meet new, different people - so you are bound to encounter new, different points of views. Feel free to disagree with those and argue why, but Apero.Social is definitely not a place to attack or harass others over these differences.
Do I have to drink?
Not at all! Obviously feel free to have a glass of whatever you want, but please do not see Apero.Social as a heavy drinking or party outlet. It is perfectly fine to have a glass of tea, sparkling water, or whatever else makes you happy.
